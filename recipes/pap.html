Pap Recipe

Description

Pap is a common household meal in West Africa. It is easy to prepare and mostly served to babies, elderly and sick people.

Ingredients

  • Corn Pap
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix Corn Pap with cold water until it is of smooth consistency.
  2. Pour hot boiling water till it thickens.
  3. Pour into a serving bowl and garnish with Sugar, Sliced Apple, Blueberries, Strawberries and milk.
